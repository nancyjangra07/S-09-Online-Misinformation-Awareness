 S-09: Online Misinformation Awareness Campaign for Young Adults

📖 Project Overview

This project focuses on addressing the growing issue of **online misinformation** among young adults (18–25 years). With the rapid spread of fake news, health myths, and manipulated media through platforms like WhatsApp and social media, this initiative aims to build **media literacy and critical thinking skills**.

 🎯 Objectives

* Assess baseline awareness and susceptibility to misinformation
* Design and execute a **multi-format awareness campaign**
* Conduct **interactive workshops on fact-checking tools**
* Develop an **open-access fact-checking resource guide**
* Measure improvement in verification behavior post-campaign

 👥 Target Audience

* **Primary:** University students (18–25 years)
* **Secondary:** Local college youth

 🤝 Stakeholders

* Student media/journalism clubs (content & outreach)
* Faculty (guidance & supervision)
* Fact-checking organizations (content validation)

 🛠️ Project Components

 1. Research

* Study types of misinformation:

  * Satire
  * Fabricated content
  * Manipulated media
* Frameworks used:

  * SIFT (Stop, Investigate, Find, Trace)
  * CRAAP Test
  * UNESCO Media & Information Literacy (MIL)

 2. Survey

* Baseline + Post-campaign survey
* Measures:

  * Misinformation awareness
  * Fact-checking behavior
  * Platform usage habits

 3. Awareness Campaign

 📱 Digital

* Instagram reels (myth vs fact)
* Informational posts & carousels
* WhatsApp stickers

 🏫 Offline

* Posters across campus
* QR code linking to resources
* Awareness booths

 4. Workshops

 Workshop 1: Fact-Checking Tools

* Reverse Image Search
* Website credibility analysis
* Tools: Google Lens, Snopes, BOOM

 Workshop 2: Advanced Topics

* Deepfake detection
* WhatsApp misinformation
* Emotional manipulation tactics

 5. Resource Guide

Includes:

* Fact-checking steps
* Trusted websites
* Common misinformation patterns
* Quick verification checklist
